## Coverage Notes

This documentation package was prepared in May 2026 based on a full analysis of the OXP/Terraf codebase. All features documented reflect the actual implementation verified in the source code.

### Modules Covered
- Authentication & account management
- Community posts, comments, likes, follows, reports
- B2B inquiries and lead management
- Product catalog with variants and dynamic attributes
- Shopping cart and checkout (guest and registered)
- Order management and tracking
- CMS: articles, materials, homepage sections
- Email center and transactional email templates
- Moderation, governance, and reporting
- Admin panel (Filament 5) — all 33+ resources and 16 pages
- Settings and configuration system
- Media/file upload and Azure Blob Storage
- Multilingual support (EN / KO / ZH)
- Deployment, environment variables, and maintenance

### Known Limitations Documented
See [15-release-notes-and-handover.md](./en/15-release-notes-and-handover.md) for the complete list of partial implementations and recommended next steps.
