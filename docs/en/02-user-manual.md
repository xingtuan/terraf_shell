# 02 — User Manual

## Introduction

This manual is written for end users of the OXP platform — customers, community members, and visitors who interact with the public website, store, and community hub.

No technical knowledge is required. The platform is designed to be intuitive and accessible.

---

## 1. Visiting the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to the OXP website URL provided by your platform operator. The homepage will load automatically.

[Screenshot placeholder: OXP homepage]

The homepage includes:
- A hero section with the brand headline and call-to-action buttons
- A "Why It Matters" section explaining the material's significance
- Business pillars and value propositions
- A material showcase section
- Community ideas preview
- A section showing collaboration and partnership opportunities
- A final call-to-action

---

## 2. Switching Language

The platform is available in **English**, **Korean (한국어)**, and **Simplified Chinese (简体中文)**.

To switch language:
1. Find the **language switcher** in the top navigation bar (typically shown as a globe icon or language abbreviation).
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown.
3. The page will reload in the selected language.

Your language preference is reflected in the URL (e.g., `/en/`, `/ko/`, `/zh/`).

## 5. Articles

Articles are educational and informational posts created by the platform team. To browse articles:
1. Click **Articles** in the navigation or look for an article section on the homepage.
2. Browse the article list (sorted by publish date).
3. Click any article title to read the full content.

Articles include a cover image, reading time estimate, and rich text content.

## 6. Store

### 6.1 Browsing Products

1. Click **Store** in the navigation bar.
2. Browse the product grid.
3. Use the **category filter** on the left (or top on mobile) to filter by product category.
4. Use the **search bar** to search for specific products by name or keyword.
5. Use **sort options** to order by price, newest, or relevance.

[Screenshot placeholder: Store product grid with filters]

### 6.2 Product Detail Page

Click on any product card to open the product detail page. You will find:

- **Product name and description** (in your selected language)
- **Product images** — click thumbnails to switch the main image
- **Product variants** — select size, color, or other options if available
- **Price** — displayed in NZD (New Zealand Dollars) with GST included
- **Stock status** — shows whether the item is available
- **Features and care instructions**
- **Material benefits**
- **Certifications**
- **Shipping and return notes**
- **Product FAQs**
- **Add to Cart** button (or inquiry button for inquiry-only products)
- **Material Request** button (if enabled for this product)

[Screenshot placeholder: Product detail page]

### 6.3 Adding to Cart

1. On the product detail page, select a product variant if prompted (size, color, etc.).
2. Click **Add to Cart**.
3. A cart sidebar will appear confirming the item was added.
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout.

### 6.4 Viewing and Updating the Cart

Click the **cart icon** in the top navigation to open the cart sidebar, or navigate to `/store/cart`.

From the cart you can:
- View all items with quantities and prices
- Increase or decrease quantities using the `+` and `−` buttons
- Remove items using the remove button
- See the subtotal
- Proceed to checkout

### 6.5 Checkout

> **Note**: Both guest and registered user checkout are supported.

**To checkout as a guest:**
1. From the cart, click **Proceed to Checkout**.
2. Enter your email address and shipping details.
3. Select a shipping method (standard or express, or rural surcharge if applicable).
4. Review your order summary.
5. Enter payment information (payment gateway integration is configured by the operator).
6. Place your order.

**To checkout as a registered user:**
1. Make sure you are logged in.
2. Add items to cart and click **Proceed to Checkout**.
3. Select a saved address or enter a new one.
4. Select shipping method.
5. Review and place your order.

### 6.6 Order Confirmation

After successfully placing an order:
- You will see an **order confirmation page** with your order number (format: `OXP-XXXXXX`).
- A confirmation email may be sent if email is configured.
- You can track your order using the order number.

### 6.7 Tracking Your Order

**As a guest:**
1. Navigate to **Store → Orders** or go directly to `/store/orders`.
2. Enter your email address and order number.
3. View the order status and items.

**As a registered user:**
1. Go to **Account → Orders**.
2. Find your order in the list.
3. Click the order to view full details and status.

### Order Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Order received, awaiting confirmation |
| **Confirmed** | Order confirmed by the store |
| **Processing** | Order is being prepared |
| **Shipped** | Order has been dispatched |
| **Delivered** | Order has been delivered |
| **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled |

## 7. Account Registration

To create an account:
1. Click **Sign Up** or **Register** in the navigation.
2. Fill in your **name**, **username**, **email address**, and **password**.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Check your email for a **verification link** and click it to verify your address.
5. Once verified, you can log in.

> **Note**: Usernames must be unique. Passwords must meet minimum security requirements.

---

## 8. Login

1. Click **Log In** in the navigation.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Log In**.

If you forget your password, click **Forgot Password?** on the login page to request a password reset email.

---

## 9. Account Dashboard

After logging in, your account dashboard is accessible via the top navigation. Your dashboard shows:

- Welcome message with your username
- Quick links to orders, community activity, addresses
- Account status notices (if your account has any restrictions)

### 9.1 Profile Management

Go to **Account → Profile** to:
- Update your **display name**, **bio**, and **location**
- Upload or change your **avatar** (profile picture)

### 9.2 Account Settings

Go to **Account → Settings** to:
- Change your **email address**
- Change your **password**

### 9.3 Address Management

Go to **Account → Addresses** to:
- View saved shipping addresses
- Add a new address
- Edit existing addresses
- Set a default address (used automatically at checkout)
- Delete addresses

### 9.4 Order History

Go to **Account → Orders** to:
- View all your past orders
- Click any order to see full details including items, quantities, prices, and current status

## 10. Community

### 10.1 Browsing Community Posts

1. Click **Community** in the navigation.
2. Browse the post feed. Posts are sorted by recency or trending score.
3. Use the **search bar** to find posts by keyword.
4. Use **category filters** to view posts in specific categories.
5. Click any post title or card to read the full post.

[Screenshot placeholder: Community hub with post feed]

### 10.2 Reading a Post

A post detail page shows:
- Post title, author, and publication date
- Cover image (if any)
- Rich text content with embedded media
- Attached images or documents (if any)
- Funding link (if the post includes a campaign)
- Like button and like count
- Save / bookmark button
- Comment section

### 10.3 Creating a Post (Registered Users Only)

> You must be logged in to create posts.

1. Click **Create Post** or the post composer button.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** (required)
   - **Content** — use the rich text editor to write formatted content, add headings, lists, and links
   - **Cover image** — upload an image to be shown at the top of the post
   - **Category** — select the most relevant category
   - **Tags** — add relevant tags to improve discoverability
   - **Media attachments** — attach up to 12 files (images, PDFs, documents) or up to 4 external links
3. Click **Publish** to submit the post.

> **Note**: Depending on platform settings, posts may be published immediately or go through a moderation review before appearing publicly.

### 10.4 Editing and Deleting Your Post

- To edit: open your post, find the edit button (available to post authors), and modify the content.
- To delete: open your post and select the delete option. Deleted posts are removed permanently.

### 10.5 Liking a Post

Click the **heart / like icon** on any post to like it. Click again to unlike. Like counts are shown publicly.

### 10.6 Saving a Post

Click the **bookmark icon** to save a post to your personal saved list. Access your saved posts from **Account → Community → Saved**.

### 10.7 Commenting

1. Scroll to the comment section at the bottom of any post.
2. Type your comment in the text box.
3. Click **Post Comment** to submit.
4. To reply to a specific comment, click **Reply** under that comment.

You can edit or delete your own comments.

### 10.8 Following Users

Visit another user's profile by clicking their username anywhere on the platform. On their profile page, click **Follow** to follow them. Followed users' new posts may appear in your feed.

### 10.9 Notifications

Click the **notification bell icon** in the navigation to view your notifications. You will be notified when:
- Someone likes your post
- Someone comments on your post
- Someone replies to your comment
- Someone follows you
- A system announcement is posted

### 10.10 Reporting Content

If you encounter content that violates community guidelines, you can report it:
1. Click the **report** option (typically in a three-dot menu on a post or comment).
2. Select the reason for your report.
3. Submit the report.

Reports are reviewed by moderators and administrators.

## 11. B2B Inquiry

If you are a business looking to wholesale, partner, or request review packs:

1. Navigate to **B2B** or **Contact** in the navigation or footer.
2. Fill in the structured inquiry form with:
   - Company information
   - Contact details
   - Type of inquiry (wholesale, distribution, manufacturing, custom production, training)
   - Description of your needs
3. Submit the form.
4. A confirmation message will appear. The platform team will review your inquiry and contact you.

---

## 12. Contact Form

For general inquiries:
1. Navigate to **Contact**.
2. Fill in your name, email, subject, and message.
3. Submit the form.

## 14. Common Error Messages

| Message | What It Means |
|---|---|
| "Please log in to continue" | You need to be authenticated to perform this action |
| "Your account is restricted" | Your account cannot create posts or comments; contact support |
| "Your account has been suspended" | Your account is temporarily disabled |
| "This post is unavailable" | The post has been removed or is pending review |
| "Item out of stock" | The selected product variant has no remaining inventory |
| "Invalid or expired link" | Your password reset or email verification link has expired — request a new one |
